# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Creative directors, marketing managers, and event organizers seeking a high-end experience/event design agency.

## Product Purpose
Showcase "Take Me Live's" expertise in creating immersive, culturally impactful live experiences through a cinematic, scroll-driven portfolio.

## Brand Personality
Bold, immersive, creative, impactful, "culture-moving."

## Anti-references
Standard/template corporate layouts, slow performance, cluttered interfaces, stock-heavy design, generic contact forms.

## Design Principles
1. **Scroll as Narrative**: The scroll interaction *is* the story.
2. **Motion-as-Function**: Animations (GSAP) are never just decorative; they guide the user and emphasize content.
3. **Typography-First**: Oversized, dramatic typography carries the voice.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Maintain WCAG contrast standards. All GSAP animations must have `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` alternatives (e.g., crossfades instead of motion) to ensure accessibility for users with vestibular disorders.
